Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This policy explains what information Dawn (the iOS app) collects, why, and how you can control it. Dawn is made by Jaco, an independent software studio. If you have questions, write to support@jacomakes.com.

What we collect

  • Account info. When you sign in with Apple, we receive a stable user identifier and (optionally) the email you choose to share. We do not receive your real name unless you provide it.
  • Photos you capture. Each daily reading photo is stored privately under your account. Photos are visible only to you.
  • Usage data. Entry dates, streak length, and the wake hour you set. This is what makes the habit-tracking work.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active Pro entitlement, processed by RevenueCat on behalf of the App Store.

What we do not collect

  • We do not run analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not access your photo library beyond saving the cards you explicitly choose to save.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any of your data.

Third-party processors

Dawn uses two third-party services to deliver core functionality. Each acts as a data processor on our behalf and is bound by their own privacy policy.

  • Supabase (Supabase, Inc.) — provides authenticated storage for your reading photos, optional reflection notes, and account session. Data is scoped to your account via row-level security; we do not retain or inspect your photos server-side beyond what your account permissions allow. See supabase.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat (RevenueCat, Inc.) — handles subscription receipts and entitlement state for the in-app subscription. It receives a pseudonymous user identifier and the App Store receipt; it does not receive your reading photos, notes, or sign-in identifiers beyond the App Store transaction. See revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • Apple — App Store, Sign in with Apple, and Push Notifications. Subject to Apple's own privacy terms.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not sell or rent your data.

How long we keep it

As long as your account exists. You can delete everything at any time from Settings → Delete account inside the app. Deletion is immediate and permanent: photos, entries, and the account itself are removed from our database within minutes.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or have it deleted at any time. Email support@jacomakes.com.

Children

Dawn is rated 4+ on the App Store but is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we update this policy materially, we will update the date at the top and post a notice in the app.
