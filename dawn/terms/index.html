Service description

Dawn is an iOS application that helps you build a daily morning reading habit, kept honest by a live photo. The core mechanic is:

  • You set a personal wake hour the night before.
  • Each morning, before that wake hour, you open the app and capture a live photo of your open book or reading material.
  • Capturing a photo before the deadline increments your streak by one day. Missing the deadline resets your streak to zero. The day cannot be backfilled.
  • The app records your daily entries, displays a streak history, a calendar of past days, and a shareable "Daily Card" that combines your photo with the date and day number.

Dawn is provided as a consumer mobile application distributed through the Apple App Store. Some features (habit tracking, the camera capture flow, reminders, and on-device history) are available without a paid subscription. Advanced features (unlimited entries, cloud backup, sharing) require an active Pro entitlement (see Subscriptions). We may add, change, or remove features over time as the product evolves.
